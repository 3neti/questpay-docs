# Format Options

---

## Overview

QuestPay™'s flexible format can be adapted to different media platforms, audience sizes, and production budgets. Each format offers unique advantages for sponsors, audiences, and producers.

---

## TV Broadcast Series

### Description

Traditional television production with 10-12 episodes per season, shot over 6-8 weeks with professional production crew.

### Format Details

- **Episode Length**: 45-60 minutes
- **Episodes per Season**: 10-12
- **Contestants**: 20-50 (with eliminations)
- **Coverage**: National broadcast
- **Production Timeline**: 3-6 months from concept to air

### Advantages

✅ Maximum reach and credibility  
✅ Premium sponsor positioning  
✅ High production value  
✅ Broadcast ad revenue opportunities  
✅ Syndication and rerun potential  

### Best For

- Launch markets with strong TV viewership
- Premium brand positioning
- Attracting tier-1 sponsors
- Building franchise credibility

### Production Partners

TV5, GMA, ABS-CBN, CNN Philippines, or regional broadcasters

---

## Livestreamed Social Competition

### Description

Real-time streaming on YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, or Twitch with continuous coverage of contestant progress.

### Format Details

- **Stream Duration**: 4-8 hours continuous or segmented daily
- **Total Duration**: Weekend or week-long events
- **Contestants**: 10-30
- **Coverage**: Social media platforms
- **Production Timeline**: 4-8 weeks prep

### Advantages

✅ Direct audience interaction  
✅ Lower production costs  
✅ Viral potential  
✅ Youth demographic engagement  
✅ Real-time sponsor integration  

### Best For

- Youth-focused campaigns
- Quick turnaround productions
- Testing new concepts
- Maximizing social engagement

### Technology Requirements

- Multi-camera mobile streaming setup
- Real-time graphics and overlays
- Chat moderation tools
- Live transaction feed integration

## Seasonal City Events

### Description

Weekend or multi-day public events where participants compete in a specific geographic area.

### Format Details

- **Event Duration**: 1-3 days
- **Participants**: 50-500 (open registration)
- **Coverage**: City or regional
- **Format**: Self-paced or scheduled waves
- **Production Timeline**: 6-8 weeks

### Advantages

✅ Community engagement  
✅ In-person brand activation  
✅ Local sponsor opportunities  
✅ Repeatable format  
✅ Lower media costs  

### Best For

- Local tourism boards
- City-specific sponsors
- Community banking initiatives
- Festival tie-ins

### Example Events

- **Manila Urban Quest** - Metro-wide exploration
- **Cebu Island Challenge** - Tourist destination focus
- **Davao Green Quest** - Environmental theme
- **BGC Fintech Rally** - Business district competition

Quests designed around new product features or services

Cause-related challenges supporting corporate social responsibility goals

## Hybrid Formats

### TV + Social Integration

Combine broadcast episodes with continuous social media engagement:

- Main show on TV weekly
- Daily social media challenges between episodes
- Live voting and audience participation
- Extended content on streaming platforms

### Multi-City Franchise

Simultaneous competitions in multiple cities:

- Local qualifiers in each city
- Regional champions
- National finals with top performers
- Cross-city rivalries and collaboration

### Audience Participation Layer

Viewers compete in parallel challenges:

- "Play along from home" quests
- Prediction games with prizes
- Social media side missions
- Viewer-voted challenges for contestants

## Format Selection Guide

### Choose TV Broadcast If:

- You have secured major network partner
- Budget exceeds $500k per season
- Goal is maximum reach and prestige
- Targeting mainstream audience

### Choose Livestream If:

- Targeting youth demographic (18-35)
- Need quick turnaround (under 3 months)
- Budget under $200k
- Prioritizing social engagement and virality

### Choose City Events If:

- Focus on local community
- Multiple local sponsors lined up
- Testing format before scaling
- Budget $50k-150k per event

### Choose Brand Sponsorship If:

- Single sponsor ready to fund fully
- Specific product/service promotion
- Measuring direct conversion/ROI
- Budget flexible based on scope

### Choose Corporate If:

- Targeting B2B revenue
- Building pipeline of repeat customers
- Professional audience research
- Budget per event $100k-1M+

## Format Evolution Roadmap

### Phase 1: Proof of Concept

**Format**: City Event + Livestream  
**Goal**: Validate mechanics and audience interest  
**Duration**: Single weekend  
**Investment**: $50k-100k

### Phase 2: Pilot Season

**Format**: 5-episode livestream series  
**Goal**: Build audience and sponsor interest  
**Duration**: 5 weeks  
**Investment**: $200k-500k

### Phase 3: Full Launch

**Format**: TV broadcast series  
**Goal**: Establish format credibility  
**Duration**: 10-12 weeks  
**Investment**: $1M-3M

### Phase 4: Franchise Expansion

**Format**: Multiple formats simultaneously  
**Goal**: Scale nationally and internationally  
**Duration**: Year-round operation  
**Investment**: $5M-10M annually
