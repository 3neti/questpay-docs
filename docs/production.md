# Production Plan

---

## Phased Rollout

### Phase 1: Pilot (Season 0)

**Goal**: Proof-of-concept in Metro Manila with 5-stage mini-quest

**Timeline**: 3-4 months  
**Investment**: $450k-750k  
**Contestants**: 10-15  
**Episodes**: 3-5

**Investment Breakdown**:
- Core production: $200k-500k
- x-Change platform license: $200k
- Year 1 maintenance: $50k

**Deliverables**:
- Working game mechanics
- Technology validation
- Audience interest data
- Sponsor pitch materials

---

### Phase 2: Season 1

**Goal**: Full broadcast edition, national coverage

**Timeline**: 6-8 months  
**Investment**: $1M-3M  
**Contestants**: 20-50  
**Episodes**: 10-12

**Deliverables**:
- Prime-time TV series
- National brand awareness
- Proven sponsor ROI
- Format documentation for franchise

---

### Phase 3: Franchise Rollout

**Goal**: City- or country-based editions, licensed globally

**Timeline**: Ongoing, Year 3+  
**Investment**: Variable by market  
**Formats**: Multiple simultaneous productions

**Deliverables**:
- Regional versions
- International adaptations
- Special editions and spinoffs

## Production Timeline

### Pre-Production (Months 1-3)

**Month 1: Development**
- Finalize format and rules
- Secure primary sponsors
- Payment provider setup (wallet partner or Netbank fallback)
- Location scouting

**Month 2: Casting**
- Launch registration campaign
- Process applications
- Conduct auditions
- Select contestants

**Month 3: Prep**
- Contestant orientation
- Challenge design and testing
- Production crew hiring
- Technology setup and testing

### Production (Months 4-5)

**Filming**: 6-8 weeks

**Weekly Schedule**:
- Monday-Tuesday: Challenge setup
- Wednesday-Friday: Principal filming
- Weekend: Contestant rest, crew prep

**Per Episode**:
- 3-5 days filming
- 100+ hours raw footage
- Multiple camera crews
- Real-time monitoring

### Post-Production (Months 6-8)

**Month 6-7: Editing**
- Rough cuts
- Story editing
- Graphics and effects
- Sound design

**Month 8: Delivery**
- Final cuts
- QC and compliance
- Broadcast masters
- Marketing materials

## Production Budget (Season 1)

| Category | Cost | % of Total |
|----------|------|------------|
| **Crew & Talent** | $300k-500k | 25-30% |
| **Equipment & Technology** | $200k-300k | 15-20% |
| **Contestant Prizes & Support** | $150k-250k | 12-15% |
| **Locations & Permits** | $100k-150k | 8-10% |
| **Post-Production** | $150k-200k | 12-15% |
| **Marketing & Promotion** | $200k-300k | 15-20% |
| **Overhead & Contingency** | $100k-200k | 8-12% |
|| **TOTAL** | **$1.2M-1.9M** | **100%** |

**Note on Payment Provider Costs**:
- **With Wallet Partner**: Typically covered through revenue-share or co-marketing arrangement (no direct cost)
- **With Netbank (fallback)**: ₱15 per transaction (~$10k-30k estimated for Season 1 based on transaction volume)
