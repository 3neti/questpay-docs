# Sponsorship & Partnerships

---

## Partner Types

| Type | Example | Role | Investment Level |
|------|----------|------|------------------|
| **Primary Wallet Sponsor** | Maya, GCash, UnionBank | Power all transactions, primary branding | $500k-2M/season |
| **Merchant Sponsors** | 7-Eleven, Grab, Lazada | Provide challenge locations, product placement | $50k-200k/season |
| **NGO / CSR Partners** | DSWD, UNDP, Ayala Foundation | Host impact missions, cause alignment | In-kind + $25k-100k |
| **Media Partners** | TV5, GMA, YouTube | Broadcast and distribution | Revenue share |
| **Technology Partners** | Cloud providers, dev tools | Infrastructure and platforms | In-kind |

## Brand Activation Opportunities

### Title Sponsorship

**"QuestPay™ Powered by [Brand]"**

**Benefits**:
- Logo on all materials
- Opening/closing sponsor mentions
- Exclusive wallet partner status
- Transaction data insights
- First right of renewal

**Investment**: $1M-3M per season

### Episode Sponsorship

**"Tonight's Quest brought to you by [Brand]"**

**Benefits**:
- Episode naming rights
- Branded challenges
- Product placement
- Commercial spots
- Social media features

**Investment**: $50k-150k per episode

### Challenge Sponsorship

**Individual challenges branded by sponsors**

**Benefits**:
- Challenge location at sponsor venue
- Branded quest objectives
- Logo on challenge materials
- Social media mentions
- Transaction attribution

**Investment**: $10k-50k per challenge

### Merchant Integration

**Sponsor locations as challenge venues**

**Benefits**:
- Foot traffic generation
- Brand exposure on broadcast
- Transaction data
- Content creation rights
- Co-marketing opportunities

**Investment**: $25k-100k + in-kind

## Brand Integration Examples

### Financial Services

**Example**: Leading Bank  
**Integration**: "Open a savings account and deposit your challenge winnings"  
**Value**: New account acquisitions, youth engagement

### FMCG/Retail

**Example**: Convenience Store Chain  
**Integration**: "Purchase 3 items totaling exactly ₱99"  
**Value**: Store visits, brand visibility, sales lift

### Telecommunications

**Example**: Mobile Network  
**Integration**: "Top-up your phone and send load to 3 friends"  
**Value**: Load sales, customer activation

### E-Commerce

**Example**: Online Marketplace  
**Integration**: "Order and pay for delivery to mystery location"  
**Value**: App downloads, transactions, logistics showcase

### Food & Beverage

**Example**: Restaurant Chain  
**Integration**: "Buy the 'Quest Meal' and scan receipt"  
**Value**: Promotional sales, location visits

## Sponsorship Packages

### Platinum Package ($1.5M-3M)

- Title sponsorship
- Exclusive category rights
- 4 branded challenges per episode
- Full data access
- Co-branded merchandise
- Premiere event hosting

### Gold Package ($500k-1M)

- Presenting sponsorship
- 2 branded challenges per episode
- Commercial spots
- Social media integration
- Limited data access

### Silver Package ($150k-300k)

- Episode sponsorship (3-5 episodes)
- 1 branded challenge per episode
- Logo placement
- Social mentions

### Bronze Package ($50k-100k)

- Challenge sponsorship (5-10 challenges)
- Merchant partner status
- Basic brand exposure

## Measurement & ROI

### Sponsor KPIs

| Metric | Measurement Method |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Brand Awareness** | Pre/post surveys, social listening |
| **Transaction Volume** | Direct attribution via codes |
| **New Customers** | App downloads, registrations |
| **Engagement** | Social metrics, website traffic |
| **Media Value** | Earned media tracking |
| **Sales Lift** | Point-of-sale data analysis |

### Reporting

**Weekly**:
- Transaction volume
- Social media metrics
- Viewership data

**Monthly**:
- Detailed analytics
- ROI calculations
- Optimization recommendations

**End of Season**:
- Comprehensive report
- Case studies
- Renewal proposal
