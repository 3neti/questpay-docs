# Monetization

---

## Revenue Streams

| Stream | Description | Estimated % of Total Revenue |
|--------|-------------|------------------------------|
| **Sponsorship Fees** | Per episode or season from corporate brands | 50-60% |
|| **Redemption Revenue** | Small % cut per QuestPay voucher redemption | 10-15% |
| **Licensing Rights** | IP licensing for regional or themed editions | 15-20% |
| **Advertising & Media Rights** | Broadcast ad slots, streaming royalties | 10-15% |
| **Merchandising** | Digital badges, collectibles, branded gear | 2-5% |
| **Corporate Events** | B2B team-building experiences | 5-10% |

---

## Revenue Projections

### Pilot Season (5 episodes)

**Costs**: $200k-500k  
**Revenue**: $300k-700k  
**Profit Margin**: 20-40%

**Sources**:
- Primary sponsor: $200k-400k
- Episode sponsors: $50k-150k
- Redemption fees: $20k-50k
- Licensing/other: $30k-100k

### Full Season 1 (12 episodes)

**Costs**: $1M-2M  
**Revenue**: $1.5M-4M  
**Profit Margin**: 30-50%

**Sources**:
- Title sponsor: $800k-1.5M
- Episode sponsors: $400k-800k
- Merchant partners: $150k-300k
- Redemption fees: $100k-200k
- Media/advertising: $50k-200k

### Mature Operation (Year 3+)

**Multiple seasons + franchise**

**Annual Revenue**: $10M-20M  
**Annual Profit**: $4M-10M

---

## Transaction Fee Model

### Per-Voucher Fee

**Structure**: 1-2% of voucher value OR flat fee per redemption

**Example**:
- ₱100 voucher = ₱1-2 fee
- ₱1000 voucher = ₱10-20 fee

**Volume**: 10,000-50,000 redemptions per season

**Revenue**: $10k-200k per season (scales with audience participation)

---

## Licensing Model

### Format Licensing

**Territory Rights**: Exclusive rights to produce QuestPay™ in specific geography

**Fee Structure**:
- Upfront license fee: $50k-200k
- Ongoing royalty: 5-10% of gross revenue
- Technical support package: $20k-50k

**Target Markets**:
- Southeast Asia (6 countries)
- Latin America (5 countries)
- Africa (3 countries)

**Potential**: $500k-2M annual licensing revenue at scale

## Corporate B2B Model

### Team-Building Packages

|---------|-------|--------|

## CSR & NGO Funding

### "Gamified Giving" Initiatives

**Model**: Partner with NGOs and foundations for cause-themed editions

**Revenue**:
- Grant funding for production: $50k-200k
- Matched donations from viewers: $20k-100k
- Corporate CSR budgets: $50k-150k

**Impact**: Generates revenue while creating positive social impact
