# Selection Process

---

## Overview

The QuestPay™ selection process is designed to identify contestants who will create compelling television, represent diverse demographics, and successfully navigate redemption-based challenges.

---

## Selection Criteria

### Technical Readiness (30%)

- **Wallet Verification**: Active, verified mobile wallet account
- **Warm-Up Completion**: Successfully completed onboarding challenge
- **Technical Literacy**: Demonstrates comfort with digital transactions
- **KYC Status**: Passed all compliance requirements

### On-Camera Potential (25%)

- **Application Video Quality**: Engaging, authentic, clear communication
- **Personality**: Charismatic, relatable, camera-comfortable
- **Storytelling**: Compelling personal narrative or motivation
- **Uniqueness**: Distinctive perspective or background

### Social Engagement (20%)

- **Application Reach**: Shares, views, and reactions on application materials
- **Following**: Social media presence (not required, but considered)
- **Engagement**: Interaction with campaign content
- **Influence Potential**: Likelihood to drive viewer engagement

### Diversity & Representation (15%)

- **Geographic Distribution**: Balance across regions
- **Demographic Mix**: Age, gender, occupation variety
- **Experience Level**: Mix of digital payment novices and power users
- **Background Diversity**: Various life experiences and perspectives

### Random Element (10%)

- **Luck Factor**: Element of chance to ensure fairness
- **Accessibility**: Provides opportunity regardless of advantages
- **Surprise**: Introduces unpredictability and excitement

## Selection Stages

### Stage 1: Application Review

**Timeline**: Days 1-7 after registration closes

**Process**:

1. Automated filtering removes incomplete or ineligible applications
2. KYC and wallet verification status checked
3. Applications segmented by geographic region
4. Initial scoring applied based on objective criteria

**Output**: Top 500-1000 applications advance

### Stage 2: Warm-Up Challenge

**Timeline**: Days 8-14

**Process**:

1. Shortlisted applicants receive onboarding code
2. Must complete simple payment challenge
3. System tracks completion time and accuracy
4. Provides data on technical readiness

**Output**: Top 200-300 applicants advance

### Stage 3: Video/Application Assessment

**Timeline**: Days 15-21

**Process**:

1. Production team reviews application videos
2. Scoring based on on-camera presence
3. Story producer evaluates narrative potential
4. Casting director identifies standout personalities

**Output**: Top 100 applicants invited to auditions

### Stage 4: Auditions

**Timeline**: Days 22-30

**Formats**:

- **Video Auditions**: For applicants outside main production city
- **In-Person Auditions**: For local applicants or top candidates

**What Happens**:

- 5-10 minute interview on camera
- Sample challenge completion
- Personality assessment
- Background verification initiated

**Output**: Final 40-60 semi-finalists identified

### Stage 5: Final Selection

**Timeline**: Days 31-37

**Process**:

1. Producers review all audition footage
2. Balance checks (demographics, geography, etc.)
3. Background checks completed
4. Contract negotiations initiated
5. Final 20-50 contestants selected

**Output**: Official cast announced

## Scoring Rubric

### Application Video (25 points)

| Criteria | Poor (1-5) | Average (6-15) | Excellent (16-25) |
|----------|-----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Quality | Shaky, unclear audio | Decent framing, audible | Professional, well-lit, clear |
| Content | Generic, unfocused | Clear message, some personality | Compelling, unique, memorable |
| Authenticity | Scripted, stiff | Somewhat natural | Genuine, charismatic |

### Warm-Up Challenge (20 points)

- **Completion**: 10 points (pass/fail)
- **Speed**: 5 points (percentile ranking)
- **Follow-Through**: 5 points (quality of participation)

### Social Engagement (15 points)

- **Shares**: Up to 5 points
- **Views/Reach**: Up to 5 points
- **Engagement Rate**: Up to 5 points

### Diversity Contribution (15 points)

- **Geographic**: 5 points (filling needed regions)
- **Demographic**: 5 points (age, gender, occupation balance)
- **Narrative**: 5 points (unique perspective or background)

### Audition Performance (25 points)

| Criteria | Poor (1-8) | Average (9-17) | Excellent (18-25) |
|----------|-----------|----------------|-------------------|
| On-Camera | Uncomfortable, nervous | Adequate, improving | Natural star quality |
| Quick Thinking | Struggles with improv | Handles basics | Sharp, strategic, creative |
| Likability | Off-putting, closed | Neutral, pleasant | Charismatic, engaging |

## Diversity & Inclusion Policy

### Goals

QuestPay™ is committed to representing the full diversity of our audience:

- **50% minimum women** (target: balanced representation)
- **Geographic spread** across urban, suburban, and rural areas
- **Age diversity** from 18-60+ years
- **Occupation variety** (students, professionals, entrepreneurs, retirees)
- **Experience levels** from digital payment novices to experts

### Equity Considerations

- Provide accommodations for persons with disabilities
- Offer auditions in multiple languages if applicable
- Consider economic diversity (challenges designed for various budgets)
- Ensure accessibility regardless of smartphone model/quality

## Audition Guidelines

### For Video Auditions

**Requirements**:

- 5-10 minutes maximum
- Good lighting and audio
- Unedited, single take preferred
- Answer provided questions
- Show personality, not perfection

**Questions** (typically 3-5 of these):

1. Tell us about yourself and why you want to join QuestPay™
2. Describe your experience with digital payments
3. What's your strategy for winning?
4. Tell us about a challenge you've overcome
5. How would you use the prize money?
6. What makes you different from other applicants?

### For In-Person Auditions

**Format**:

- 10-15 minute session
- Interview with casting team
- Brief payment challenge simulation
- Camera test and photo
- Q&A opportunity

**What to Bring**:

- Valid government ID
- Smartphone with active wallet
- Enthusiasm and energy!

## Notification Process

### For Selected Contestants

**Initial Contact** (Day 35):

- Phone call from production team
- Email with next steps
- Contract and NDA sent for review

**Onboarding** (Days 36-42):

- Contract signing
- Orientation session
- Production schedule shared
- Contestant portal access granted
- Final prep materials provided

### For Non-Selected Applicants

**Notification** (Day 38):

- Thank you email sent
- Invitation to be a Side Player
- Encouragement to apply for future seasons
- Special discount/offer from sponsors

**Waitlist** (5-10 applicants):

- Held as backups if selected contestants drop out
- Notified of status
- Must remain ready to participate on short notice

## Legal & Compliance

### Contestant Agreements

All selected contestants must sign:

1. **Participation Agreement**
   - Outlines rules, expectations, and obligations
   - Specifies prize structure and payment terms
   - Defines grounds for disqualification

2. **Media Release**
   - Grants rights to film, photograph, and broadcast
   - Allows use of name, likeness, and story
   - Covers promotional materials and future use

3. **Non-Disclosure Agreement**
   - Prohibits revealing results before broadcast
   - Protects production details and strategies
   - Enforceable with financial penalties

4. **Code of Conduct**
   - Expected behavior during production
   - Social media guidelines
   - Sponsor relationship protocols

### Verification Requirements

- **Background Check**: Criminal record screening
- **Credit Check**: Not scored, but financial fraud reviewed
- **Social Media Audit**: Public posts reviewed for controversial content
- **Reference Checks**: Personal or professional references contacted

## Contestant Support

### Pre-Production Support

- **Training Session**: How the game works, tips and strategies
- **Technical Support**: Wallet troubleshooting, app guidance
- **Production Prep**: What to expect on filming days
- **Wardrobe Guidance**: What to wear, what to avoid

### During Production

- **Production Coordinators**: On-site support staff
- **Medical Staff**: First aid and health monitoring
- **Transport & Logistics**: Getting contestants to challenges
- **Safety Protocols**: Emergency procedures and contacts

### Post-Production

- **Media Training**: How to handle interviews and social media
- **Community Management**: Support with fan interactions
- **Alumni Network**: Connection with past contestants
- **Future Opportunities**: Casting calls for special editions
